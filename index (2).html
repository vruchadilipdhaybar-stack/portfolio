Revolutionizing Airfare Estimation: AI Powered Co-Pilot

Vrucha Dhaybar · Rachana Ravindra Mudholkar · Vishal Borate · Sachin Yashwant Zurange · Poonamkumar S Hanwate · Brijesh Vishwakarma

Abstract

The 5G cellular wireless technology is a digital revolution of ultra-reliability, low-latency and has enormous numbers of devices connected and data rates that are written records. This text includes an overall analysis of the critical security issues of 5G architecture — expansion of attack surface, network slicing threats, IoT attacks, and supply chain risks. It introduces mitigation practices including AI-based threat detection, blockchain authentication, and edge computing security, presenting a new Hybrid AI-Blockchain Security Algorithm (HABSA) for adaptive and scalable security.
